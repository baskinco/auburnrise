Lake Life

A private lake is something few people get to enjoy. And with a home in Auburn Rise, the lake will be yours, all year long. Enjoy water activities all summer, then gather for skating with family and friends in the winter. Live lake life – to its fullest.

Convenience

Life in Auburn Rise gives you effortless access to everything you want, and need. Shop at the new Seton Urban Development, and never go far for groceries and other goods. Plus, Deerfoot Trail, Stoney Trail, and the new LRT are just minutes away.

Socialize

Auburn Rise is a community where you can get together with friends, connect and get the most out of your free time. Auburn Rise offers a better quality of life — starting with your social life.
