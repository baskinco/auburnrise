The Logel Story

Tim Logel, President and Founder of Logel Homes is a leader in the development and construction of multifamily communities. With over 35 years of experience, he’s worked on the sale and construction of over 6000 single family homes and 2500 condos and townhomes. With a continued focus on exceeding customer expectations and leadership in innovation, Tim welcomes you to Auburn Rise, the inaugural Logel Homes development in Auburn Bay.

Whether you’re buying your first home, or right-sizing for the future, you’ll see value and quality in every part of your Logel Homes condo. As an award-winning multifamily developer, we’re driven to constantly improve, innovate and exceed the ever-changing needs of homebuyers. We offer greater value and more standard high end finishings - all in the very best locations.
