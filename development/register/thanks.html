About Logel Homes

When it comes to multifamily homes, we’re raising the standards – standards Tim Logel learned from seeing the hard work and vision that went into his father’s organic cattle business decades ago. Today, that same vision defines the Logel experience.

Whether you’re buying your first home, or right-sizing for the future, excellence comes standard in every Logel home. With first class multi-family developments, we’re driven to constantly improve, innovate and exceed the ever-changing needs of homebuyers. We offer more standard high end finishings and give you more for your dollar - all in the very best locations.

With decades of experience under the Cardel Lifestyles brand, Logel Homes is now building under its own name while continuing to offer the same great townhomes and condos.
