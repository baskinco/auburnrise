CUSTOMIZE

Logel Homes is proud to offer some of the home building industry's best standard specifications; however, we love providing our homeowners with the flexibility to truly make their home their own. Explore the customization possibilities for taking your condo to the next level.

APPLIANCES

PackagesImprove your cooking experience with premium upgrade packages that include a stainless-steel wall oven, cooktop, built-in microwave, front control electric range, induction range option, and a chimney style hood fan.

Individual AppliancesAdd your own personal flair with individual appliance upgrades, including options for your washer, dryer, fridge, range, microwave, hood fan, wall oven, and dishwasher.

COUNTERS & CABINETS

CountertopsChoose from three granite colour options for your kitchen and bathrooms.

Make your kitchen the focal point of your home with options for a thicker Quartz countertop profile and waterfall-edge islands.

Eating SpaceCreate space for entertaining with an extended island eating area (as per plan).

CabinetryPersonalize your cabinets with additional colours, styles, and modern hardware.

Customize even further with two-tone cabinet options, where lower cabinets are a separate colour from the upper cabinets, or the island cabinetry is unique from the rest of the kitchen.

StorageCreate more storage in your kitchen by extending the height of your cabinets, adding in a gourmet pantry (as per plan), and selecting large pot drawers.

Compliment your bathroom spaces with a medicine cabinet and vanity drawers.

Stay organized with custom shelving layouts for all closets and laundry rooms.

PLUMBING

Sinks Upgrade your kitchen sink with a single or double bowl under-mount sink in stainless steel or Silgranit®.

Elegant under-mount bathroom sink option.

FaucetsSelect from several modern plumbing fixture upgrade packages, including single-hand pull-down kitchen faucets and modern 2-handle bathroom faucets.

ShowerCreate your own oasis with a deluxe shower stall featuring fully tiled walls and a sliding frameless door with 10mm glass.

GarburatorUpgrade your kitchen sink with a garburator for added convenience.

WALLS & FLOORING

BathroomsChoose from a number of tile options for your bathroom walls, floors, and vanity backsplashes.

Enhance your bathroom with floating mirrors on a fully tiled vanity wall (as per plan).

KitchensCustomize your kitchen backsplash with modern tile options.

Vinyl FlooringIncrease the comfort of your home with Wicanders® Vinyl Comfort Flooring featuring a cork underlay and heightened durability.

Premium Carpet OptionsBring a unique vibe to your unit with additional carpet colour options.

Add SmartCushion underlay to all carpeted areas to improve energy efficiency and create a quieter environment in your unit.

WINDOWS & LIGHTING

Custom BlindsBring your own style and sophistication to your new home with custom blind options for all of your windows.

Kitchen LightingIlluminate your space with unique pendant light options, modern light fixture packages, and functional under-cabinet lighting.

OTHER UPGRADES

Cooling & Heating Cozy up with a wall-mounted electric fireplace or cool down with an air conditioning unit.

OutletsBoost your space with horizontal plugs and USB outlet options.

BBQ Gas LineA gas line to the BBQ brings convenience to your outdoor cooking.
